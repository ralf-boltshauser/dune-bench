Spice Collection Phase
This is the eighth Phase of the Turn. Forces in territories that contain spice may collect the spice. +1.13.01
HARVESTING SPICE: Any player whose Forces Occupy a Sector of a Territory in which there is spice may now collect that spice. This is done by taking the spice tokens you are entitled to from the Territory and Putting them behind your shield.
COLLECTION RATE: The collection rate of spice for each Force is 2 spice per Force. If the player occupies Carthag and/or Arrakeen their collection rate is now 3 spice per Force.
UNCLAIMED SPICE: Uncollected spice remains where it is for future turns.
