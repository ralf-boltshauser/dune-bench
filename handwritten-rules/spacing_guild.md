Spacing Guild
When playing Basic rules use only abilities 2.06.01 through 2.06.11. When playing Advanced Rules use abilities 2.06.01 through 2.06.12.00.
Starting Spice [0.12]: Put 5 spice behind your shield from the bank.
Starting Forces [0.13]: Place 5 Forces in Tuek's Sietch and 15 Forces in reserves (off-planet).
FREE REVIVAL: 1 Force.
PAYMENT FOR SHIPMENT: When another faction ships Forces onto Dune, they pay the spice to you instead of to the Spice Bank.✷ -3.01.11.03
THREE TYPES OF SHIPMENT: You are capable of making one of three types of shipments each Turn.
NORMAL SHIPMENT: You may ship normally from off-planet reserves to Dune.
CROSS-SHIP: You may ship any number of Forces from any one Territory to any other Territory on the board.✷
OFF-PLANET: You may ship any number of Forces from any one Territory back to your reserves.✷
HALF PRICE SHIPPING: You pay only half the normal price (rounded up) when shipping your Forces.✷
RETREAT CALCULATIONS: The final price of your Forces shipped back to reserves is 1 spice for every 2 Forces.
SPACING GUILD SPECIAL VICTORY CONDITION: If no faction has been able to win the game by the end of play, you have prevented control of Dune and automatically win the game.
ALLIANCE: Your ally may use the ability HALF PRICE SHIPPING [2.06.06].✷
ALLIANCE: Your ally may use the ability CROSS-SHIP [2.06.05.01].✷
ALLIANCE: Your ally wins with you when you win with the Spacing Guild Special Victory Condition [2.06.08].
✷SHIP AS IT PLEASES YOU: During the Shipment and Movement Phase you may activate either ability SHIP AND MOVE AHEAD OF SCHEDULE [2.06.12.01] or HOLDING PATTERN [2.06.12.02].✷ -3.03.05.02
The rest of the factions must make their shipments and movements in the proper sequence. You do not have to make known when you intend to make your shipment and movement action until the moment you wish to take it.
SHIP AND MOVE AHEAD OF SCHEDULE: You may take your shipment and move action before any player earlier in storm order than you. This would allow you to go first, or after any player has taken their complete Shipment and Movement action. -3.03.05.02
HOLDING PATTERN: When you are up next in storm order you may announce, "Delay". You may take your shipment and move action after any player later in storm order than you. This would allow you to go last, or after any player has taken their complete Shipment and Movement action. -3.03.05.03
