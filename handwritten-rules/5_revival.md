Revival Phase
This is the fifth Phase of the Turn. All players are allowed to revive Forces and leaders from the Tleilaxu Tanks. There is no Storm Order in this Phase.
Force Revival: All players may now revive up to 3 Forces from the Tleilaxu Tanks. -1.14.08 -2.03.07
To revive Forces you must adhere to the following:
FREE REVIVAL: A certain number of Forces are revived for free as stated on the player sheet. +2.04.11
BY SPICE: Any additional Forces that may be revived, beyond your free revival and up to your current limit, must be done at a cost of 2 spice per Force.
PAYMENT: All spice paid for Force Revival is Placed in the Spice Bank.
TO RESERVES: Revived Forces must be Put in the player's reserves.
Leader Revival: When a player has no Active Leaders or all of there leaders have died at least once, they may revive 1 face up leader per Turn until all of their leaders have been revived. +1.14.02 -1.14.08 -3.01.20
To revive leaders you must adhere to the following:
COST: For a leader, a player must pay that leader's fighting strength in spice to revive that leader.
PAYMENT: All spice paid for leader revival is Placed in the Spice Bank.
REVIVED LEADER STATUS: A revived leader is added to that player's Leader Pool and can be played normally, it is still subject to being a Traitor.
DEAD AGAIN: When a revived leader is killed again, place it face down in the Tleilaxu Tanks. This leader can not be revived again until all the player's other revivable leaders have been revived, killed, and sent to the Tleilaxu Tanks again / are unavailable. -1.14.02 -3.01.20
FACE DOWN LEADERS: At anytime through out the Turn you may look at any face down leader with your matching faction symbol.

Fremen
When playing Basic rules use only abilities 2.04.01 through 2.04.12. When playing Advanced Rules use abilities 2.04.01 through 2.04.21.
Starting Spice [0.12]: Put 3 spice behind your shield from the bank.
Starting Forces [0.13]: Place 10 Forces distributed as you like on Sietch Tabr, False Wall South, and False Wall West; and 10 Forces in reserves.
NATIVES: Your Reserves are in a Territory on the far side of Dune (in front of your shield, off the board). Unlike other factions you do not have Off-Planet Reserves and can not ship with the normal Shipping method.
FREE REVIVAL: 3 Forces.
SHIPMENT: During the Shipment [1.06.03], you may Send any or all your reserves for free onto the Great Flat or onto any one Territory on the Map within two territories of the Great Flat (subject to storm and Occupancy Limit). This ability costs 1 shipment action to use.
MOVEMENT: During movement you may Move your Forces two territories instead of one.✷
SHAI-HULUD: When Shai-Hulud appears in a Territory where you have Forces, they are not devoured.✷
BEAST OF BURDEN: Upon conclusion of the Nexus you may ride the sandworm and Move some or all the Forces in the Territory, as long as they are not in Storm, to any Territory without allied Forces subject to storm and occupancy limits. Any Forces in that Territory are not devoured. If Shai-Hulud appears again and you still have Forces in the original Territory, you may do this again.
FREMEN SPECIAL VICTORY CONDITION
When the Spacing Guild is in the game and no faction has won by the end of the last turn your plans to alter Dune have succeeded, you and your allies win the game if you meet the following criteria
Only your Forces (or no Forces) occupy Sietch Tabr and Habbanya Sietch
Neither Harkonnen, Atreides, nor Emperor Forces occupy Tuek's Sietch.
ALLIANCE: You may decide to protect (or not protect) your allies from being devoured by sandworms.✷
ALLIANCE: At your discretion, your ally's free revival is 3.✷
ALLIANCE: Your allies win with you when you win with the Fremen Special Victory Condition (ability 2.04.09).
STORM RULE: Move the Storm Marker normally using the Battle Wheels on the first Turn of the game. You randomly select a card from the Storm Deck and Put it face down on the margin of the game board. In the next Storm Phase the number on that Storm Card is Revealed; the storm is moved counterclockwise that number of Sectors and your Storm Card is returned to the Storm Card Deck. You then shuffle the Storm Deck, randomly select a Storm Card for the next turn's storm movement, and Put it face down on the margin of the game board. -3.01.23
✷THERE'S A STORM COMING: At the end of the Storm Phase [1.01] you may secretly look at the Storm Card.
SANDWORMS: During Spice Blow [1.02], all additional sandworms that appear after the first sandworm in a Spice Blow can be Placed by you in any sand Territory you wish. Any Forces there, except yours, are devoured.✷
STORM LOSSES: When your Forces are caught in a storm, only half of them there are destroyed (rounded up). A storm landing on your Forces then moving in a subsequent Turn does not destroy half of your Forces a second time.✷
STORM MIGRATION: You may Send your reserves into a storm at half loss.✷
✷FEDAYKIN: Your three starred Forces, Fedaykin, have a special fighting capability. They are worth two normal Forces in battle and in taking losses.
FEDAYKIN REVIVAL: They are each treated as one Force in revival.
FEDAYKIN TRAINING: Only one Fedaykin Force can be revived per Turn.
✷BATTLE HARDENED: Your Forces do not require spice to count at full strength in battles.

Emperor
When playing Basic rules use only abilities 2.03.01 through 2.03.07. When playing Advanced Rules use abilities 2.03.01 through 2.03.11.
Starting Spice [0.12]: Put 10 spice behind your shield from the bank.
Starting Forces 0.13: Place 20 Forces in reserves (off-planet).
FREE REVIVAL: 1 Force.
PAYMENT FOR TREACHERY: During Buying A Card [1.04.06.02], when any other faction pays spice for a Treachery Card, they pay it to you instead of the Spice Bank.✷
FAIR MARKET: You may not discount the price of Treachery Cards; the full price must be paid.
ALLIANCE: You may give spice to your ally at any time for any reason. Spice you give to your ally goes behind their shield and is now their spice.
ALLIANCE: You may pay spice for the revival of up to 3 extra of your ally's Forces beyond their current limit from the Tleilaxu Tanks.✷
✷SARDAUKAR: Your five starred Forces, elite Sardaukar, have a special fighting capability. They are worth two normal Forces in battle and in taking losses against all opponents except Fremen.
SARDAUKAR WEAKNESS: Your starred Forces are worth just one Force against Fremen Forces.
SARDAUKAR REVIVAL: They are treated as one Force in revival.
SARDAUKAR TRAINING: Only one Sardaukar Force can be revived per Turn.

Atreides
When playing Basic rules use only abilities 2.01.01 through 2.01.09. When playing Advanced Rules use abilities 2.01.01 through 2.01.15.
Starting Spice [0.12]: Put 10 spice behind your shield from the bank.
Starting Forces [0.13]: Place 10 Forces in Arrakeen and 10 in reserves (off-planet).
PROPER MANAGMENT: After Player Positions [0.10] Put the Spice Deck and the Treachery Deck near your player position. You manage these decks.
FREE REVIVAL: 2 Forces.
✷BIDDING: During the Bidding Phase when a Treachery Card comes up for purchase, you may look at it before any faction bids on it.
FILAMENT BOOK: You, and only you, may keep written records about Treachery cards.
✷WORMSIGN: During Shipment and Movement Phase [1.06], before anyone ships, you may look at the top card of the Spice Deck.
PRESCIENCE: Before Battle Wheel [1.07.04.01], before any elements of the Battle Plan are determined you may force your opponent to Reveal your choice of one of these elements they intend to use in their Battle Plan against you: the leader, the weapon, the defense, or the number dialed. If you choose to ask about a weapon or defense and your opponent tells you that they are not playing that element during this battle, you may not then ask to see a different element.✷  +3.04.01 +3.04.03
ALLIANCE: In your ally's battle you may use ability Prescience [2.01.08] on your ally's opponent.✷
THE SLEEPER HAS AWAKENED: The Kwisatz Haderach card starts out inactive and the Kwisatz Haderach token may not be used. Use the Kwisatz Haderach card and counter token to secretly keep track of Force losses. Once you have lost 7 or more Forces in a battle or battles, the Kwisatz Haderach token becomes active for the rest of the game.
✷KWISATZ HADERACH: In your battle you may add the Kwisatz Haderach token to your leader or Cheap hero's strength (+2) in one Territory per Turn. If the leader or Cheap Hero is killed the Kwisatz Haderach does not add its +2 strength.
ATREIDES LOYALTY: A leader accompanied by Kwisatz Haderach can not turn traitor.
PROPHECY BLINDED: The Kwisatz Haderach token can only be killed if blown up by a lasgun/shield explosion.
REAWAKEN: When killed, the Kwisatz Haderach must be revived like any other leader. When all other leaders have died once and/or become unavailable you may use your one leader revival action to revive this token instead of a leader. -1.14.08
ASCENSION: Alive or dead, the Kwisatz Haderach does not prevent the Atreides from reviving leaders.
