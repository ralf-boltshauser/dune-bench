# 1.05 Revival Phase

This is the fifth Phase of the Turn. All players are allowed to revive Forces and leaders from the Tleilaxu Tanks. There is no Storm Order in this Phase.

## 1.05.01 Force Revival
All players may now revive up to 3 Forces from the Tleilaxu Tanks. -1.14.08 -2.03.07

To revive Forces you must adhere to the following:

### 1.05.01.01 FREE REVIVAL
A certain number of Forces are revived for free as stated on the player sheet. +2.04.11

### 1.05.01.02 BY SPICE
Any additional Forces that may be revived, beyond your free revival and up to your current limit, must be done at a cost of 2 spice per Force.

### 1.05.01.03 PAYMENT
All spice paid for Force Revival is Placed in the Spice Bank.

### 1.05.01.04 TO RESERVES
Revived Forces must be Put in the player's reserves.

## 1.05.02 Leader Revival
When a player has no Active Leaders or all of there leaders have died at least once, they may revive 1 face up leader per Turn until all of their leaders have been revived. +1.14.02 -1.14.08 -3.01.20

To revive leaders you must adhere to the following:

### 1.05.02.01 COST
For a leader, a player must pay that leader's fighting strength in spice to revive that leader.

### 1.05.02.02 PAYMENT
All spice paid for leader revival is Placed in the Spice Bank.

### 1.05.02.03 REVIVED LEADER STATUS
A revived leader is added to that player's Leader Pool and can be played normally, it is still subject to being a Traitor.

### 1.05.02.04 DEAD AGAIN
When a revived leader is killed again, place it face down in the Tleilaxu Tanks. This leader can not be revived again until all the player's other revivable leaders have been revived, killed, and sent to the Tleilaxu Tanks again / are unavailable. -1.14.02 -3.01.20

### 1.05.02.05 FACE DOWN LEADERS
At anytime through out the Turn you may look at any face down leader with your matching faction symbol.
